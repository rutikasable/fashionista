Body Types

Rectangle: Balanced bust and hips with a less defined waist. This body type often has a straight silhouette with minimal curves.

Hourglass: Well-defined waist with balanced bust and hips. This shape is characterized by a curvy silhouette with a narrow waist.

Pear: Hips wider than bust with a defined waist. This body type typically has a smaller upper body and fuller hips and thighs.

Apple: Bust wider than hips with less defined waist. This shape often features a fuller midsection and slimmer legs.

Inverted Triangle: Broad shoulders with narrower hips. This body type has a wider upper body and a more tapered lower body.
